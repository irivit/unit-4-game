Crystal Colector

Instructions:

You will be give a random number at the beginning. By clicking on a Crystal you will add points to your total score. You will win the game by matching your total score to random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each Crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the value of each Crystal will change.
